04 · Architecture

Three Access Tiers. One Venue-Anchored Graph.

Layer 1 · Visitor · Free

Walk In. Scan. Done.

  • QR scan at venue entrance or bar counter
  • Name · Mobile OTP · Email (30 seconds)
  • "What's your scene tonight?" — one-tap intent
  • Live room: see who's present right now
  • Session closes when you leave the venue
  • No app install required. Browser-native.
Layer 2 · Member · Phase 2

Verified. Persistent. Cross-Venue.

  • Persistent verified identity across all venues
  • Full profile: role, city, intent history
  • Cross-venue connection history and graph
  • Access to member-only events and mixers
  • IPNOTEC identity layer integration
  • Pricing to be validated in Phase 1 data
Layer 3 · Venue Partner · Phase 2

Venue Intelligence Dashboard

  • Real-time foot traffic and scene analytics
  • Anonymised professional profile of visitors
  • Event creation and job/opportunity posting
  • Repeat visit tracking and retention data
  • One SPOC per venue. Full onboarding support.
  • Pricing to be validated in Phase 1 data

Phase 1 deploys Layer 1 only. We intentionally keep it free, frictionless, and observable. We measure real human behaviour before attaching any monetisation layer to it.

09 · Roadmap

Phase 1 → Phase 2: What Changes and Why

Phase 1 · Now → Day 90
Validate Behaviour
Prove that strangers with shared physical presence will voluntarily connect through a venue-based layer. No monetisation. No complexity. Maximum observability.
  • 10 Bengaluru venues. All categories: bar, café, coworking, club
  • Layer 1 only. Free. OTP-verified. Browser-native. No app install.
  • Manual operations. Founder-led venue onboarding.
  • Zero revenue targets. 100% learning targets.
  • Weekly data review. Venue health checks.
  • Community seeding: 3 strategic partner orgs send their members
  • Tech stack: simple HTML/Node.js MVP. Lean. Deployable in days.

Exit Condition for Phase 2: ≥700 users, ≥500 connections, ≥6 venues retained at Day 90. If hit → activate Phase 2. If not → extend or pivot.

Phase 2 · Q3 2026 → Q1 2027
Monetise Proven Behaviour
Once behaviour is validated, introduce monetisation in sequence. Each revenue layer only activates after the prior layer's adoption is confirmed by data.
  • Member subscription activated (pricing informed by Phase 1 data)
  • Venue partner dashboard with analytics and posting rights
  • Events marketplace: Founder Nights, Investor Mixers, Speed Networking
  • City HQ concept space: one physical command center in Bengaluru
  • Live city screen: which venues are active right now
  • IPNOTEC identity layer integration for persistent cross-city profile
  • Expand to 30+ venues. Target 5,000 members.

Phase 2 unlock requires Phase 1 exit conditions to be met. Revenue numbers, City HQ, and membership pricing are intentionally absent from Phase 1 materials. They are earned, not assumed.
